Blend information

  • Name: Star Wars Sith Fury
  • Author: Rocz3D.
  • Premalink: http://www.blendswap.com/blends/view/74105.
  • For Blender 2.52 and up.
  • Description:

    This is a high poly model of the Sith Fury player vehicle from the Star Wars the Old Republic video game.

    The model used Cycles materials, but the te xture images are packed with the .blend.

    The model is rigged:

    -- control to open/close the back wings

    --rotate gun turrets

    --shap ekeys for the landing gear hatch covers

    --simple armatures for the landi ng gear and boarding ramp.

    The landing gear, boarding ramp and hatch cov ers animations are NLA blocks, so you can just position those animation bits whe rever you need them in your timeline.

    The text file included in the .ble nd details how the rig works.

    Enjoy!
